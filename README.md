# ⚡ WakaCharge

**Powering Learning. Empowering Students.**

WakaCharge is a smart clean energy platform that enables students to rent solar-powered power banks and rechargeable study lamps through a digital application. Built to address the challenge of unreliable electricity in Nigeria, WakaCharge combines renewable energy, QR-code technology, and secure digital payments to provide affordable, on-demand portable energy.

## 🚀 Problem

Millions of Nigerian students experience frequent power outages that interrupt online classes, delay assignments, and reduce study time. Many also rely on commercial charging points, paying daily fees while risking the safety of their devices.

## 💡 Solution

WakaCharge provides a digital platform where students can:

- Create an account
- Fund their wallet
- Rent a power bank or rechargeable study lamp
- Scan a QR code to collect a device
- Return the device at any designated WakaCharge station

Our solar-powered smart kiosks make reliable energy accessible, affordable, and sustainable.
