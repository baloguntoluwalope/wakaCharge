# ⚡ WakaCharge

demolink: https://wakacharge.vercel.app

> **Powering Learning. Empowering Students.**

WakaCharge is a smart clean energy platform designed to solve the challenge of unreliable electricity for students in Nigeria. Through a QR-enabled Progressive Web App (PWA), students can rent portable power banks and rechargeable study lamps from solar-powered kiosks, ensuring uninterrupted learning and productivity.

Built for the **DevCareer × Nomba Hackathon 2026**, WakaCharge combines renewable energy, digital payments, and smart device management into one seamless platform.

---

## 🌍 The Problem

Power outages are a daily reality for millions of Nigerian students. They interrupt online classes, delay assignment submissions, reduce study time, and force students to pay high fees at commercial charging stations while leaving their devices with strangers.

WakaCharge was created to provide a safer, smarter, and more affordable alternative.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

WakaCharge provides an end-to-end portable energy rental platform where students can:

- Create an account
- Fund their digital wallet
- Receive a virtual account
- Scan a QR code
- Rent a power bank or rechargeable study lamp
- Return the device to an operator or smart kiosk

Our long-term vision is to build Africa's leading digital infrastructure for campus utilities, starting with energy.
